London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. It is known for its fashion, art, and culture. The city is also famous for its landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Paris has a rich history that dates back more than two thousand years. It has been a center of government, learning, and commerce since the Middle Ages.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital city of Japan. It is one of the largest cities in the world, known for its modern skyscrapers, pop culture, and technological advancements.

Tokyo has a vibrant history and has been the political, economic, and cultural center of Japan for centuries.
